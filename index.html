I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering, IIT Guwahati, Guwahati, India, in the area of wireless power transfer (WPT) system for EV applications.

Thesis Supervisor: Dr. ing. Praveen Kumar, Professor, Dept. of EEE, IIT Guwahati.

Interests

My research interests include the analysing and designing the wireless charging coils and developing the power electronic circuits in WPT system for charging of EVs. Besides, the design and development of the smart solar home system for rural areas in India. Furthermore, I am interested in developing mainly two wheeler EV, utilizing the existing one. At my leisure, I used to exploring the technology advancements in the related area that helps me to refine the research work.
