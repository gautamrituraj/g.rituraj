Gautam Rituraj, Ph.D.

Alumni of E-Mobility Lab, Department of EEE, IIT Guwahati-781039, Assam, India.

g.rituraj@alumni.iitg.ac.in, gautamrtrj91@gmail.com

I earned my PhD degree at the Electronics and Electrical Engineering (EEE) Department, IIT Guwahati, India. I developed various approaches to maximizing the transmission efficiency (TE) and power transfer capability (PTC) of the wireless power transfer (WPT) system for EV charging applications, which are well documented in my PhD thesis. Presently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Technische Universiteit Delft , The Netherlands in DC systems, Energy Conversion and Storage group, and working on off-grid EV charger (plug-in).

Thesis Supervisor: Dr.ing. Praveen Kumar, Professor, Dept. of EEE, IIT Guwahati.

Interests

Current research interests include the design and analysis of off-grid EV chargers (plug-in). It also includes analysing and designing the wireless charging coils and developing the power electronic circuits in the WPT system for charging EVs. Moreover, I am interested in developing charging strategies for wired and wireless charging systems to extend the battery life. Besides that, it includes the design and development of the smart solar home system with multiple charging strategies for rural areas in India. At my leisure, I explored the technological advancements in the related field that helped me refine the research work.
