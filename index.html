g.rituraj@alumni.iitg.ac.in, gautamrtrj91@gmail.com

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Technische Universiteit Delft, Netherlands, in DC systems, Energy Conversion and Storage group. Before joining TU Delft, I finished my PhD degree at the Electronics and Electrical Engineering (EEE) Department, IIT Guwahati, India. Here, I worked on various approaches to maximising the transmission efficiency and power transfer capability of the wireless power transfer (WPT) system for EV charging applications, which are well documented in my PhD thesis.

My current research interests include software development for the integration of hybrid energy storage systems (heat and electricity), design and analysis of off-grid EV chargers (plug-in), standalone microgrid design, and digital twin for electrolyzer systems. It also includes analyzing and designing the wireless charging coils (magnetics) and developing the power electronic circuits in the WPT system for charging EVs. Besides that, it consists of designing and developing a smart solar home system with multiple charging strategies for rural areas in India.
